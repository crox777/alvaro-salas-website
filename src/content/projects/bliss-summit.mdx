## Fight Club for Freedom

BLISS Summit is an annual invitation-only gathering held in Nosara, Costa Rica. Each edition brings together approximately 100 leaders from diverse backgrounds — philanthropists, family office principals, technologists, artists, activists, and dissidents — united by a commitment to advancing freedom worldwide.

## Impact

By its 4th edition in 2026, BLISS has catalyzed over **$100 million in capital** deployed to freedom initiatives. The summit operates on a model of radical trust and confidentiality, creating a space where leaders can speak freely, form partnerships, and commit resources without the performative dynamics of traditional conferences.

## The 2025 Theme: "The Truth We Seek"

The most recent edition focused on disinformation and polarization — exploring how the erosion of shared truth undermines democratic institutions and what can be done to rebuild information integrity.

## The Moonshot

BLISS Summit's long-term goal is ambitious: **grow democracy funding from less than 1% to 10% of U.S. philanthropy within a decade.** This requires shifting the mental model of freedom from a cause to an investment, and BLISS serves as the convening ground where that shift happens.
