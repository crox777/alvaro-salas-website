## Making Freedom Investible

The Reynolds Foundation and Reynolds Family Office, under Alvaro Salas-Castro's leadership as President & CEO, is one of the most active private foundations focused on advancing freedom and democratic governance worldwide.

## By the Numbers

- **90+ organizations** funded across the freedom ecosystem
- **62+ countries** reached
- **$60M+** deployed to freedom and democracy initiatives

## Key Grants & Partnerships

### Cornell Brooks School of Public Policy
- **$1.25M** (2024) and **$2.1M** (2026) for the Center on Global Democracy
- Reynolds Leadership Scholarships for emerging democracy leaders
- Bitcoin & Technology Policy Initiative (BTPI) — studying crypto adoption in 12 authoritarian-leaning countries and its potential to strengthen financial freedom

### Columbia SIPA
- Strategic partnership for democratic resilience research
- Reynolds Journalism Fellows of Latin America — sponsoring 11 Latin American journalists for investigative reporting training at Columbia

### Freedom Ecosystem
The Foundation operates as a strategic hub, connecting grantees, convening leaders through BLISS Summit, and deploying capital through the Reynolds Freedom Accelerator. Rather than passive grantmaking, the approach is active, data-driven freedom investment.
