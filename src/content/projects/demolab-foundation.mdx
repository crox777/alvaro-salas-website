## Civic Innovation from Costa Rica

DemoLab Foundation is a civic innovation think tank founded by Alvaro Salas in Costa Rica. It serves as a laboratory for testing and scaling ideas that strengthen freedom, civic participation, and democratic governance.

## Programs

### Freedom Curriculum
Developed in partnership with the Human Rights Foundation network, the Freedom Curriculum is a comprehensive civic education program designed for deployment in both classroom and community settings. It covers democratic principles, human rights, and institutional design.

### FWD Tech
A technology program exploring how digital tools can enhance civic participation, government transparency, and democratic accountability. FWD Tech partners with technologists and civic organizations to prototype and test solutions.

### Nosara Research Project
A behavioral economics research initiative studying governance, community dynamics, and decision-making in Nosara, Costa Rica. Conducted in partnership with Dr. Tina Nabatchi (Syracuse University) and Christian Freitag, the project generates insights applicable to democratic governance at multiple scales.

### Latin American Liberty Forum
An annual convening of freedom advocates, policy makers, and civic leaders from across Latin America, organized through DemoLab to foster regional collaboration on freedom and institutional resilience.
